# Sawbone

The Sawbone is a physician, vivisectionist, battlefield surgeon, and  
biological engineer. Where others see flesh as sacred or immutable, the  
Sawbone sees a system that can be repaired, improved, weaponized, or  
completely rewritten.

A Sawbone does not cast spells. Instead, they practice questionable  
superscience: surgery, mutation, grafting, stimulants, engineered  
organs, and living constructs. Their discoveries are not miracles. They  
are procedures.

In civilized lands, Sawbones are indispensable. On the frontier, they  
are feared.

## Game Rule Information

- **Primary Ability:** Mind or Soul
    
- **Secondary Ability:** Grit
    
- **Alignment:** Any
    
- **Hit Die:** d8
    

### Class Skills (6 + Mind Modifier)

Autohypnosis, Bluff, Concentration, Craft, Diplomacy, Disable Device,  
Forgery, Handle Animal, Heal, Knowledge (Arcana), Knowledge (Dungeoneering),  
Knowledge (Local), Knowledge (Nature), Knowledge (Religion),  
Knowledge (The Planes), Listen, Perform, Profession, Ride, Search,  
Sense Motive, Spot, Survival, Use Magic Device

### Weapon and Armor Proficiency

The Sawbone is proficient with all simple weapons, light armor, and  
medical implements.

# Class Features

All of the following are class features of the Sawbone.

## Organic Science

The Sawbone practices superscience through biological engineering,  
chemical manipulation, and anatomical modification.

Instead of learning spells, the Sawbone learns **Discoveries**:  
repeatable scientific procedures that create medicines, mutations,  
living structures, grafts, stimulants, and engineered organisms.

A Discovery is not cast. It is prepared.

Preparing a Discovery generally requires:

- tools,
    
- materials,
    
- time,
    
- and access to the subject being altered.
    

Many Discoveries create persistent effects which require maintenance,  
replacement reagents, feeding, repair, or periodic recalibration.

The Sawbone gains:

- 1 Discovery at 1st level,
    
- and 1 additional Discovery every odd level thereafter.
    

Discoveries are divided into schools of superscience such as:

- Alchemy,
    
- Biollurgy,
    
- Mesmerism,
    
- Vitalism,
    
- Fleshcraft,
    
- or Vivic Engineering.
    

The GM determines which Discoveries are available in the campaign.

---

## Anatomical Expertise

A Sawbone understands the structure of living creatures with terrifying  
precision.

The Sawbone may:

- identify diseases, poisons, parasites, mutations, and injuries,
    
- determine how a creature died,
    
- identify biological weak points,
    
- and perform autopsies or forensic examination.
    

The GM must answer truthfully, though answers may require examination,  
samples, or time.

---

## Emergency Treatment

Beginning at 2nd level, the Sawbone may stabilize a dying creature as a  
Free Action once per scene.

In addition, once per encounter, the Sawbone may:

- remove nonlethal damage,
    
- stop bleeding,
    
- or restore a small amount of hit points  
    through rapid treatment.
    

---

## Surgical Alteration

At 3rd level, the Sawbone may permanently alter a helpless creature  
through surgery.

Examples include:

- scars,
    
- limb replacement,
    
- grafts,
    
- organ modification,
    
- deformities,
    
- sterilization,
    
- implanted tools,
    
- or controlled mutations.
    

These procedures are nonmagical and persist until reversed through  
further treatment.

At higher levels, these alterations become:

- faster,
    
- more complex,
    
- and increasingly inhuman.
    

---

## Combat Medic

Beginning at 4th level, the Sawbone may treat creatures during combat  
without suffering the normal penalties for rushed medical work.

The Sawbone may perform:

- diagnosis,
    
- stimulant injection,
    
- pain suppression,
    
- or battlefield surgery  
    during active encounters.
    

---

## Sphere of Influence

At 5th level, and again at 10th and 15th level, the Sawbone expands  
their field of expertise.

Choose one:

- gain access to a new field of superscience,
    
- unlock advanced Discoveries in an existing field,
    
- or develop a specialized medical discipline.
    

Examples include:

- resurrection science,
    
- engineered parasites,
    
- living architecture,
    
- mutagenics,
    
- memory surgery,
    
- or synthetic organisms.
    

---

## Constructed Servant

At 6th level, the Sawbone creates a biological servant.

This creature may be:

- vat-grown,
    
- surgically assembled,
    
- genetically engineered,
    
- or partially mechanical.
    

The servant obeys the Sawbone and can be modified through Discoveries.

The exact statistics are determined later through the servant system.

---

## Genetic Transfusion

Beginning at 7th level, the Sawbone may permanently alter hereditary  
traits.

This may include:

- bloodline modification,
    
- inherited mutations,
    
- disease immunity,
    
- alchemical adaptation,
    
- or engineered physiology.
    

These changes may pass to offspring if biologically appropriate.

---

## Revitalization

At 8th level, the Sawbone may restore creatures from the brink of death.

Once per scene, when a creature would die, the Sawbone may intervene to:

- stabilize them,
    
- restore consciousness,
    
- or temporarily suppress fatal injury.
    

The creature survives, though often with scars, complications, or  
required follow-up treatment.

---

## Advanced Surgical Alteration

At 9th level, the Sawbone’s procedures become dramatically more invasive.

They may:

- reshape anatomy,
    
- alter senses,
    
- graft creature traits,
    
- replace organs,
    
- or restructure biological function.
    

These alterations may grant both benefits and complications.

---

## Hostile Procedures

Beginning at 11th level, the Sawbone may weaponize their medical  
expertise.

This includes:

- engineered toxins,
    
- destabilizing mutagens,
    
- flesh degeneration,
    
- parasitic agents,
    
- or surgical sabotage.
    

Creatures normally immune to poison or disease are no longer completely  
safe from the Sawbone’s work.

---

## Emergency Action

At 12th level, the Sawbone may intervene in moments that should already  
be fatal.

If a creature has only recently died, the Sawbone may attempt emergency  
resuscitation through violent medical intervention.

This is not resurrection. The creature was never fully allowed to die.

---

## Biostructure Specialist

At 13th level, the Sawbone becomes an expert in living architecture and  
engineered biology.

Biological structures created or modified by the Sawbone become:

- tougher,
    
- faster-healing,
    
- and easier to maintain.
    

---

## Rapid Procedure

Beginning at 14th level, procedures that once required hours may now be  
performed in minutes.

Minor procedures may even be performed during encounters.

---

## Master Surgical Alteration

At 15th level, the Sawbone may perform legendary transformations.

Examples include:

- replacing entire body systems,
    
- creating hybrid organisms,
    
- rebuilding ruined bodies,
    
- or engineering entirely new biological functions.
    

---

## Always on Call

Beginning at 16th level, the Sawbone may use Emergency Treatment and  
Combat Medic abilities without normal cooldown restrictions.

---

## Perfected Anatomy

At 17th level, the Sawbone gains profound mastery over their own body.

They may:

- suppress pain,
    
- regulate organs consciously,
    
- resist toxins and disease,
    
- survive catastrophic injury,
    
- or continue functioning through conditions that would kill others.
    

---

## Superior Servant

At 18th level, the Sawbone’s Constructed Servant becomes a masterpiece  
of biological engineering.

It may possess:

- multiple forms,
    
- implanted Discoveries,
    
- regenerative systems,
    
- or unnatural intelligence.
    

---

## Medical Mastery

At 19th level, the Sawbone stands among the greatest physicians in the  
world.

The Sawbone may:

- fully restore a creature through treatment,
    
- reverse horrific injury,
    
- cure nearly any mundane ailment,
    
- or preserve life through otherwise impossible circumstances.
    

---

## Birth of Knowledge

At 20th level, the Sawbone achieves a breakthrough that changes the  
understanding of life itself.

Choose or create a supreme Discovery representing a revolutionary  
advance in superscience.

Examples include:

- synthetic immortality,
    
- fully artificial life,
    
- resurrection through cloned tissue,
    
- engineered species,
    
- or living cities.
    

The Sawbone becomes permanently renowned—or feared—for this achievement.
