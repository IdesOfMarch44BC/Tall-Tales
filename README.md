# Tall Tales

A tabletop roleplaying game of guns, magic, and factions on a stateless frontier — where the Wild answers territorial claims in proportion to their scale, so nobody makes them, and civilization runs on companies, guilds, registries, and promises instead of nations.

**Status: full manuscript draft.** Lore and mechanics are written in Markdown. The core engine is playable, and all ten classes now have complete power lists across Volumes 1–3; numbers remain playtest tuning stakes.

## The Game in Brief

- **d6 pools, fixed 4+ success.** Skills roll dice; the five planar **Alignments** (Heavens, Machine, Shadow, Wild, Wyrd) can augment a roll with dice or be invoked for automatic successes — always at a price.
- **Momentum.** Sides alternate surges; each surge is 1d6 of shared action economy the whole side spends together.
- **Powers known.** Powers are always available once learned. Each has a level from 0–10 and spends a listed mix of Alignment dots from a personal reserve that refreshes whenever the character’s side takes the Momentum.
- **Wounds, not hit points.** Damage compares to thresholds; wounds roll location and effect; majors can always kill. The frontier costs you an arm before it costs you your life — and sells it back at interest.
