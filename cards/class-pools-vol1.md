# Class Card Pools, Volume 1

## Witness

### The Line-Holder

- **Shield Up** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — +1 Defend until your side’s next surge.

- **Hold Here** — 1 — L1 — Stance — While you don’t move, allies within 2 tiles gain +1 Defend and can’t be forced to move.

- **You Face Me** — 1 — L1 — One enemy within 3 tiles must include you in its next attack or lose 1 die from it.

- **Stand Fast** — 1 — L1 — Reaction — Negate forced movement or knockdown on you or an adjacent ally.

- **Guarded Advance** — 1 — L1 — Move 1 tile; +1 Defend this round.

- **Not One Step** — 1 — L2 — Stance — Enemies leaving your reach take a free Strike from you at +1 die.

- **Brace the Wall** — 1 — L2 — Stance — Allies adjacent to you can’t be pushed or knocked down.

- **Taunt the Pack** — 1 — L2 — Up to 2 enemies within 3 tiles must include you in their next attacks or take –1 die.

- **The Door Does Not Open** — 1 — L3 — Reaction — When an enemy tries to pass within your reach, stop its movement and Strike it.

- **Anchor Point** — 1 — L3 — This round, allies within 2 tiles may use your Defend value.

- **The Wall Answers** — 1 — L4 — Reaction — When an enemy damages an ally within 2 tiles, Strike it (+2 dice) if it’s in reach.

- **Immovable** — 1 — L4 — Stance — +1 Armor while you haven’t moved this round; immune to fear while any ally stands behind you.

- **Redoubt** — 2 — L5 — One-Shot — Until your side’s next surge, attacks on allies within 2 tiles target you instead; +2 Armor.

- **Witnessed Stand** — 1 — L5 — While you’re below half HP and holding ground: +1 die on all rolls; allies who can see you clear fear.

- **Where I Stand, the Line** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — For the scene, enemies cannot pass within 2 tiles of you without ending their movement; +1 Defend.

### The Intercessor

- **A Word of Grace** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — An ally within 5 tiles heals 1 HP or gains +1 die on their next save.

- **Spoken Plainly** — 1 — L1 — Heal an ally within 5 tiles 2 HP or clear one fear/despair effect; say aloud, in character, why they will hold.

- **Bless the Blade** — 1 — L1 — An ally’s next attack counts as Heavens-aligned and gains +1 die.

- **Keep Watch Over Them** — 1 — L1 — Stance — Allies within 3 tiles gain +1 die on saves.

- **Steady Voice** — 1 — L1 — Reaction — Cancel a fear or despair effect as it lands on an ally within 5 tiles.

- **Borrowed Light** — 1 — L2 — An ally’s next roll this round gains +2 dice; +3 if they tell the table what they’re afraid of.

- **Shared Burden** — 1 — L2 — Take up to 3 damage from an ally within 3 tiles onto yourself.

- **Litany Against the Dark** — 1 — L2 — Stance — Undead and Shadow-aligned enemies take –1 die attacking allies within 3 tiles of you.

- **Confession Heard** — 1 — L3 — Out of combat: an ally clears one lingering mental affliction and heals 2 HP; you carry what you heard.

- **Grace Under Fire** — 1 — L3 — Heal 2 HP each to two allies within 5 tiles.

- **The Vapor Carries It** — 1 — L4 — One-Shot — Ask the GM one true question about the present danger; the answer arrives as vision.

- **Stand Them Up** — 1 — L4 — Reaction — An ally dropping to 0 HP drops to 1 instead and may move 1 tile.

- **The Honest Prayer** — 1 — L5 — Your next Heavens invocation this scene gains +2 automatic successes; the price is unchanged and you know it.

- **Answered** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — Choose: all allies heal 3 HP, or one Shadow-aligned enemy takes hits equal to your Religion.

- **The Formation Prays** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — Every ally who speaks one honest sentence gains +1 die on all rolls until the scene ends.

### The Lamplighter

- **Lantern Strike** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — Strike; +1 die against undead and Shadow-aligned targets.

- **Cast Light** — 1 — L1 — Illuminate 5 tiles: hidden things are revealed; light-hating things there lose 1 Defend.

- **Lamp Oil and Iron** — 1 — L1 — Stance — Your attacks this round ignore the Armor of undead and Shadow-aligned creatures.

- **Carry the Flame** — 1 — L1 — An ally’s weapon burns clean for a round: +1 die vs Shadow-aligned targets.

- **Read the Dark** — 1 — L1 — Learn whether Shadow influence is present within 5 tiles, and roughly where.

- **No Shadow Here** — 1 — L2 — Stance — Enemies gain no benefit from darkness or concealment within 3 tiles of you.

- **Brand** — 1 — L2 — Your Strike marks the target: it sheds light, can’t hide, and takes +1 hit from Heavens-aligned attacks this scene.

- **Burn It Out** — 1 — L2 — End one ongoing Shadow effect on an ally or a tile.

- **I See You** — 1 — L3 — Name one visible enemy: your side’s attacks against it gain +1 die until it drops. It knows.

- **The Searing Word** — 1 — L3 — 2 hits to one undead or Shadow-aligned target within 5 tiles; it saves or recoils 1 tile.

- **Vigil Flame** — 1 — L4 — Stance — While you hold a light source, allies within its glow gain +1 die on saves vs Shadow and Nightmare effects.

- **Hunt the Source** — 1 — L4 — Learn the direction and rough distance of the strongest Shadow presence within the scene or site.

- **Sunrise Discipline** — 1 — L5 — Reaction — When a Shadow effect targets your side, you take it alone, with +2 dice on the save.

- **Dawn, Early** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — Burst of revealing light: enemies within 3 tiles take 2 hits (Shadow-aligned: 4); nothing within it remains concealed.

- **The Long Watch Ends** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — Against one named Shadow entity you have witnessed for a full session: this scene, your attacks against it ignore Defend and Armor.

## Drifter

### The Lone Wolf

- **Field Dressing** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — Heal an adjacent ally 2 HP.

- **Seen and Tended** — 1 — L1 — Heal an adjacent ally 3 HP; 4 if you’ve named a true thing about them this scene.

- **Walk the Rounds** — 1 — L1 — Move your full Move; each ally you pass adjacent to heals 1 HP.

- **Quiet the Panic** — 1 — L1 — Clear fear or despair from an ally within 3 tiles.

- **Comfort of the Familiar** — 1 — L1 — Stance — Allies within 3 tiles gain +1 die on morale and fear saves.

- **Get Up. You Held.** — 1 — L2 — Reaction — An ally dropping to 0 HP drops to 1 instead.

- **Tend the Flock** — 1 — L2 — Heal 1 HP to every ally within 3 tiles.

- **The Remembered Hurt** — 1 — L2 — Clear one poison, disease, or lingering injury from an adjacent ally.

- **Circuit Kit** — 1 — L3 — Consumable ×3 — Bearers may spend to heal 2 HP or reroll a save.

- **Sit With the Dying** — 1 — L3 — An ally at 0 HP stabilizes automatically and wakes with 2 HP at scene’s end.

- **Shepherd’s Eye** — 1 — L4 — Learn the true condition of everyone in sight: HP ranges, afflictions, who is lying about being fine.

- **Carry Their Weight** — 1 — L4 — Reaction — Transfer one condition or affliction from an ally within 3 tiles to yourself, at –1 severity.

- **The Flock Stands** — 1 — L5 — All allies within 5 tiles heal 2 HP and clear one condition each.

- **No One Dies Tonight** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — Until end of scene, allies within 5 tiles can’t be reduced below 1 HP by the first source that would do it each round.

- **Twenty Years on This Road** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — Out of combat: fully heal one person, body and mind — the great restoration of the circuit; the community will talk about it for a generation.

### The Living Legend

- **Look Again** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — Reroll a Perceive or Investigate roll with +1 die.

- **What Changed Here** — 1 — L1 — Ask the GM: what in this scene differs from how it should be? True answer.

- **Hold Their Gaze** — 1 — L1 — One creature must engage with you or visibly, unmistakably avoid you; either tells you something.

- **Mark the Face** — 1 — L1 — Perfectly memorize a person, mark, or document; recognize it ever after, through disguise or forgery.

- **The Rider’s Question** — 1 — L1 — +2 dice on one Convince or Investigate roll made gently.

- **Say It Plain** — 1 — L2 — One creature must answer your next question truthfully or visibly refuse.

- **Seen Through** — 1 — L2 — Reaction — When someone lies in your presence, know it; gain +1 die against them this scene.

- **Nothing Hides From Attention** — 1 — L2 — One hidden, invisible, or disguised creature within 5 tiles is revealed to you.

- **The Account Is Kept** — 1 — L3 — Stance — Enemies within 5 tiles gain nothing from concealment, disguise, or lies while you watch.

- **Testimony** — 1 — L3 — Recount what you witnessed: allies gain +1 die against a threat you’ve described, this scene.

- **The Weight of Being Seen** — 1 — L4 — One enemy that knows you’ve witnessed its acts takes –1 die on all rolls against your side this scene.

- **Read the Room Entire** — 1 — L4 — Learn every present creature’s disposition, allegiance, and immediate intent.

- **Enter It in the Book** — 2 — L5 — One-Shot — Name an enemy: for the scene your side knows its position and it can surprise no one.

- **The Unblinking Hour** — 1 — L5 — Stance — You cannot be deceived, misdirected, or surprised; illusions simply fail against you.

- **I Was There** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — Your witness becomes legally, socially, spiritually decisive: one disputed fact you personally observed is settled, everywhere it matters.

### The Road Ghost

- **Press On** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — Move your full Move as a second move this surge.

- **Road Legs** — 1 — L1 — Stance — You and adjacent allies gain +1 Move; difficult ground doesn’t slow you.

- **Know the Fords** — 1 — L1 — Automatically find the crossing, gate, or path through one terrain obstacle.

- **Saddle-Born** — 1 — L1 — Mount or dismount free; your mount uses your dice for saves.

- **Traveler’s Eye** — 1 — L1 — Assess a route or settlement at a glance: dangers, resources, mood.

- **I Know a Way** — 1 — L2 — Move an ally within 5 tiles up to their Move.

- **Ride Through** — 1 — L2 — Move your full Move ignoring enemies’ reach; +1 Defend during it.

- **The Circuit’s Pace** — 1 — L2 — Stance — Your party travels one step faster overland; you never count as lost.

- **Courier’s Burst** — 1 — L3 — An ally acts immediately after you at –1 Momentum cost (min 0 for a Move).

- **The Lantern at Dusk** — 1 — L4 — Until end of scene, allies ending their move adjacent to you heal 1 HP and clear fatigue.

- **Weather Sense** — 1 — L3 — Know the next day’s weather and one hazard on the road ahead.

- **Shortcut** — 1 — L4 — One-Shot — Your party’s next journey takes half the time; you knew a way, because you always know a way.

- **Everyone Mounts** — 1 — L5 — Your whole side gains +2 Move this round.

- **Rider on the Ridge** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — Help arrives: an NPC ally you plausibly know from your circuit enters the scene’s edge, favorably disposed.

- **The Whole Circuit Answers** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — Word goes down the roads: within days, the communities of your circuit send what you asked for — hands, supplies, shelter, or forty quiet witnesses.

## Arcanist

### The Ward-Wright

- **Warding Gesture** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — An ally within 3 tiles gains +1 Defend until your next surge.

- **Chalk Line** — 1 — L1 — A 3-tile line: enemies crossing it lose 1 die from their next action.

- **Seal the Door** — 1 — L1 — Hold one portal or passage shut (2 successes to force).

- **Damp the Working** — 1 — L1 — Reaction — Reduce one incoming supernatural effect’s hits or successes by 1.

- **Anchor Glyph** — 1 — L1 — One tile: allies standing there can’t be moved by any force.

- **Warding Circle** — 1 — L2 — A 1-tile circle: allies inside gain +1 Defend and +1 die on saves; lasts until you move.

- **Shaped Exclusion** — 1 — L2 — Choose a creature type: it cannot willingly enter one tile you ward, this scene.

- **Grounding Rod** — 1 — L2 — Stance — Supernatural effects targeting allies within 2 tiles of you lose 1 die.

- **Extend the Line** — 1 — L3 — Lengthen or reposition one of your active wards; it gains +1 to its effect.

- **Containment** — 2 — L4 — Box one enemy in force (Defend 2, HP 4 walls) for a round.

- **Layered Wards** — 1 — L4 — Stance — You may maintain two ward effects simultaneously.

- **The Quiet Hall** — 1 — L5 — A 3-tile zone: sound, panic, and fear behave conservatively — no fear effects, no surprise, inside it.

- **Ward Against the Named** — 1 — L5 — Name an enemy: your wards count double against it specifically.

- **The Vellhame Protocol** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — Suppress all supernatural effects in a 3-tile radius for one round — including your side’s.

- **The Standing Ward** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — Ward a site (a building, a camp, a bridge) for a full day: hostile supernatural forces cannot cross, and you know if anything tries.

### The Open Channel

- **Spark** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — 2 hits to one target within 5 tiles.

- **Arc** — 1 — L1 — 3 hits to one target within 5 tiles; you may take 1 damage to make it 4.

- **Flare** — 1 — L1 — 1 hit to up to 3 targets within 3 tiles.

- **Vent** — 1 — L1 — Reaction — When damaged, release: the attacker (if within 3 tiles) takes 1 hit.

- **Hold the Charge** — 1 — L1 — Your next Channel card this scene gains +1 hit.

- **Discharge** — 1 — L2 — 2 hits to every enemy in a 2-tile cone; take 1 damage per extra tile of reach you add.

- **Lash** — 1 — L2 — 2 hits to a target within 5 tiles and pull it 2 tiles toward you or push it 2 away.

- **Burn Bright** — 1 — L2 — Take 1 damage: your next attack this surge gains +2 dice or +1 hit.

- **Sustained Beam** — 1 — L3 — 2 hits to one target now and 2 more on your next surge if you don’t move.

- **Overdraw** — 1 — L4 — Stance — Your Channel cards deal +1 hit; you take 1 damage at the start of each of your surges while this holds.

- **Shatterpoint** — 1 — L4 — 3 hits to one target; on any hit, its Armor drops by 1 this scene.

- **The Mass Speaks** — 1 — L5 — Add automatic hits equal to half your level (round down) to one Channel attack; the GM notes the ease.

- **Rolling Thunder** — 2 — L5 — 2 hits to every enemy in a 3-tile line; the ground smokes.

- **The Cascade, Glimpsed** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — 6 hits, 3-tile radius, centered on you; allies included unless warded.

- **Carried Well** — 1 — L6 — One-Shot — Cancel all self-damage you would take from Channel cards this scene: tonight, the stewardship holds.

### The Book-Bound

- **Consult the Names** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×2) — Look at the top 3 cards of your holstered pile; load one (discard from hand if over 5).

- **Marginalia** — 1 — L1 — +2 dice on one Study roll; the book has notes.

- **A Familiar Passage** — 1 — L1 — Reroll any roll that duplicates one you’ve made this scene, at +1 die.

- **Chained and Carried** — 1 — L1 — Stance — Your book can’t be taken from you; attempts fail and you know who tried.

- **Bookmark** — 1 — L1 — Set aside the top card of your holstered pile face-down; load it later as a free action.

- **A Dead Master’s Trick** — 1 — L2 — Copy the printed effect of any card in your expended pile, at +1 cost.

- **Cross-Reference** — 1 — L2 — Shuffle your expended pile into holstered now.

- **The Index Knows** — 1 — L2 — Name a card in your deck: put it on top of your holstered pile.

- **The Book Remembers** — 1 — L3 — Reaction — When an enemy uses a supernatural ability, learn what it is and one weakness.

- **Borrowed Hand** — 1 — L3 — Play a card from an adjacent willing ally’s expended pile as if yours, at +1 cost.

- **Errata** — 1 — L4 — Reaction — When a card of yours fully fails, return it to your hand instead of expending it.

- **The Weather Turns** — 1 — L4 — Stance — Once per surge, your first card costs 1 less Momentum (min 1); the book is in a mood to help.

- **Read Ahead** — 1 — L5 — Look at the top 5 of holstered; reorder them.

- **Forty Hands on the Pen** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — Play up to 3 cards from your expended pile this surge, paying costs; the book is briefly awake.

- **Add Your Name** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — Commune with the housed dead: ask three questions any former holder of your book could answer. They answer. They are glad of the company.

## Dynast
