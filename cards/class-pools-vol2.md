# Class Card Pools, Volume 2

## Mentalist

### The Mind Blade

- **Drawn Will** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — Blade-Strike at +1 die.

- **First Word** — 1 — L1 — Stance — Manifest your blade: Strikes with it use Nimbleness or Athletics dice and are lethal.

- **Perfect Draw** — 1 — L1 — Reaction — Manifest and Strike an enemy that just entered your reach.

- **The Form Holds** — 1 — L1 — Stance — +1 Defend while your blade is manifest.

- **Thrown Intent** — 1 — L1 — Your blade strikes at 3 tiles and returns to your hand.

- **Cut the Material** — 1 — L2 — Your blade-Strikes this round ignore 2 Armor and sever objects outright.

- **Two Truths** — 1 — L2 — Split the will: Strike two adjacent enemies at –1 die each.

- **Edge Against the Volley** — 1 — L2 — Reaction — Cut one projectile or thrown attack against you out of the air.

- **The Long Cut** — 1 — L3 — Strike every enemy in a 2-tile line once.

- **Cut the Working** — 1 — L4 — Strike a spell, ward, or supernatural effect within reach: on 2 hits, it ends.

- **Cut the Footing** — 1 — L4 — Strike the world instead: collapse a surface, drop a beam, open the floor beneath an enemy (save or fall/prone).

- **The Blade Reads Back** — 1 — L5 — Cross blades deliberately: learn your opponent’s training, temperament, and next intention.

- **The Opened Way** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — Cut a door in distance: you and adjacent allies step up to 10 tiles, through nothing.

- **Cut What Binds** — 1 — L6 — One-Shot — Sever one intangible thing within reach: a possession, a compulsion, a curse, a leash. Not a debt. Not yet.

- **The Unnameable Edge** — 2 — L8 — One-Shot — Cut something ordinary language does not hold — an inevitability, a connection, a certainty — GM adjudicates; the scriptoria will want your account.

### The Watching Mind

- **Centered Breath** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — +2 dice on your next save, or clear a –1 die penalty on yourself.

- **Still Water** — 1 — L1 — Stance — +2 dice on saves against fear, deception, and mind-effects; you cannot be surprised.

- **Read the Stance** — 1 — L1 — One observed creature: learn its intent, its fear, or its next action.

- **Open Hand** — 1 — L1 — Read a room: every present creature’s mood and honesty, at a glance.

- **Offered Calm** — 1 — L1 — An adjacent ally gains Still Water’s benefits until your next surge.

- **Speech Without Voice** — 1 — L2 — Until end of scene, your side converses silently at any distance in sight.

- **Unmoved** — 1 — L2 — Reaction — Automatically succeed one save against fear or mental influence.

- **The Noticing** — 1 — L2 — One deception, disguise, or illusion within 5 tiles fails for you specifically.

- **Weight of Attention** — 1 — L3 — Fix one creature in your regard: it takes –1 die on all actions not directed at you.

- **Memory Palace** — 1 — L3 — Store or retrieve anything you’ve perceived with perfect fidelity; share one stored scene with a willing touch.

- **Quiet the Storm** — 1 — L4 — End one rage, panic, or frenzy effect in a creature within 3 tiles (unwilling: it saves).

- **The Shared Stillness** — 1 — L4 — Stance — Allies within 3 tiles gain +1 die on all saves and cannot be surprised.

- **Going Out** — 1 — L5 — One-Shot — Leave the body for one round: perceive anywhere within the site, unseen, then return. A rehearsal, plainly labeled.

- **The Closed Door, Held Open** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — With consent, enter a willing mind: heal one mental affliction, share one memory truly, or stand with them against something that got in.

- **The Finished Self** — 2 — L8 — One-Shot — For one scene, nothing in you is unexamined: immune to all mental influence, all your saves succeed on 3+, and you may lend one save automatically to an ally who can see your face.

### The Composed Self

- **Wayfarer’s Step** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — Move full Move ignoring difficult ground.

- **Traveling Form** — 1 — L1 — Stance — +1 Move; opportunity Strikes against you lose 1 die; your blade manifests and dismisses freely.

- **Beggar’s Bowl** — 1 — L1 — The gate custom in miniature: secure food, shelter, and a hearing anywhere the tradition is known.

- **Correcting Touch** — 1 — L1 — Adjust an ally’s grip mid-action: +1 die on their current roll.

- **Walk On** — 1 — L1 — Reaction — When missed by an attack, move 1 tile; you were already leaving.

- **A Correction, Offered** — 1 — L2 — An ally within 3 tiles rerolls up to 2 dice of a roll you watched.

- **Teaching Bout** — 1 — L2 — Spar an ally a quiet hour: they gain +1 die on one skill for the session.

- **The Road’s Judgment** — 1 — L2 — Assess a stranger as walkers do: learn their training, their trouble, and whether they’ve sat a gate.

- **The Gate Custom** — 1 — L3 — Once per scene per creature: an enemy accepting your offered pause stops fighting you until harmed.

- **Carry Word Between Houses** — 1 — L3 — Once per session: know where the nearest monastery, gate-friend, or fellow walker is, and be expected there.

- **Both Limbs** — 1 — L4 — This round, use one Edge card and one Eye card for 1 Momentum total.

- **Uninvited Nowhere** — 1 — L4 — Stance — You can’t be barred by social station: guards hesitate, doors open, courts hear you; the robe precedes you.

- **Learn What We Don’t Know** — 2 — L5 — One-Shot — Observe any technique used this scene: perform it once, now, at its printed cost.

- **The Walker’s Report** — 1 — L6 — One-Shot — Once per session: the tradition acts on your word — a house alerted, a Sundered watch begun, a Reading convened where you say.

- **Everywhere the Road Goes** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — For one journey, the party moves at walker’s pace: half time, no hazards you don’t choose, and one useful encounter the road owed you.

## Tinkerer

### The Coilwright

- **Jolt** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — 2 hits to a target within 3 tiles; constructs and Living Iron bank it instead.

- **Storage Cell** — 1 — L1 — Consumable ×2 — Spend for +1 Momentum (bearer’s surge) or to power a device for a scene.

- **Patch the Line** — 1 — L1 — Repair a device, Heart, or construct 2 HP; restore function to one dead machine.

- **Static Field** — 1 — L1 — Stance — Enemies entering tiles adjacent to you take 1 hit (vim).

- **Kill the Lights** — 1 — L1 — Cut power in a 5-tile radius: lamps, lines, and one powered device of the GM’s choice go dark.

- **Charged Weapon** — 1 — L2 — An ally’s weapon carries current: +1 hit on its next 2 hits.

- **Capacitor Trap** — 1 — L2 — Rig a tile: first enemy entering takes 3 hits (vim).

- **Ground the Line** — 1 — L3 — Reaction — Redirect one vim, lightning, or Heart-driven effect within 3 tiles into the earth; bank 1 ebb.

- **Overvolt** — 1 — L3 — A powered device or construct within 3 tiles surges: 3 hits to it, or it runs double for a round then stalls.

- **Jolt-Line Web** — 1 — L4 — String live wire across 3 tiles: enemies crossing take 2 hits and stop.

- **The Quickened Heart** — 1 — L4 — A vehicle, frame, or Heart you tend gains +1 Move and +1 die for the scene.

- **Read the Current** — 1 — L2 — Trace any powered system: layout, loads, and where it fails.

- **Rolling Blackout** — 2 — L5 — All enemy devices, constructs, and powered gear within 5 tiles lose a round.

- **The Dawn Clause** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — At the procedurally correct moment (GM agrees the sky is right): your next Coilwright card triples its numbers.

- **Heart Transplant** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — Field-swap a Constructive Heart: revive a dead great machine, or overdrive a live one to legendary output for a scene, then it rests a week.

### The Crucible-Trained

- **Flask of Opinion** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — Thrown, 5 tiles: 2 hits in a 1-tile burst; the ground is briefly burning, slick, or fogged (choose).

- **Solvent** — 1 — L1 — Destroy a door’s worth of material, or strip 2 Armor from a target until repaired.

- **Smoke Pot** — 1 — L1 — 2 tiles of dense smoke: Shoot attacks through it lose 2 dice.

- **Litmus Sense** — 1 — L1 — Identify any substance, poison, or residue by field test.

- **Quickmatch** — 1 — L1 — Reaction — Ignite, extinguish, or neutralize one small chemical event within 3 tiles.

- **Alloy on Demand** — 1 — L2 — An ally’s weapon gains +1 die or their Armor +1 until end of scene.

- **Caustic Coat** — 1 — L2 — A weapon’s next 3 hits also strip 1 Armor each.

- **Flash Powder** — 1 — L2 — 1-tile burst: enemies there save or are blinded a round (–2 dice).

- **Transmute the Ground** — 1 — L3 — 2 tiles become (choose): mire (difficult), glass (loud, slick), or brittle (collapses under weight).

- **The Stable Batch** — 1 — L3 — Consumable ×2 — A perfected flask: as Flask of Opinion but 3 hits, and an ally can throw it.

- **Neutralize** — 1 — L4 — Reaction — Cancel one acid, fire, poison, or alchemical effect as it lands, anywhere within 3 tiles.

- **Refiner’s Eye** — 1 — L4 — Assess any material’s worth, purity, and provenance; spot orichalcum through a crate.

- **The Big Batch** — 2 — L5 — 3 hits to every enemy in a 2-tile burst at 5 tiles; the crater is yours to explain.

- **Orichalcum Sliver** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — Overcharge a weapon: its next attack adds hits equal to your Study.

- **Philosopher’s Hour** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — With a bench and an hour: transmute one material into another of like mass (stone to iron, iron to glass, lead to — no. The Crucible has rules about that one).

### The Frame-Wright

- **The Frame** — 1 — L1 — Troop — Deploy your chassis: HP 5, Strike 3, Defend 1, Move 2, carries loads; re-musters between encounters.

- **Patch and Graft** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×2) — Heal your frame (or any frame/chassis) 3 HP.

- **Fetch** — 1 — L1 — The frame retrieves an object or drags an ally up to its Move.

- **Gentle the Stranger** — 1 — L1 — Calm any frame, chassis, or beast of burden; +2 dice handling it thereafter.

- **Mounted Work** — 1 — L1 — Stance — Ride your frame: your Move becomes its Move; you gain +1 Defend from height.

- **Grown for This** — 1 — L2 — Stance — The frame’s graft this scene: +1 Strike (horn), +1 Defend (plate), or +1 Move (limbs).

- **Good Beast** — 1 — L2 — Reaction — The frame interposes: an attack on you or an adjacent ally hits it instead.

- **Haul Away** — 1 — L2 — The frame moves double its Move carrying up to two people or a wagon’s load.

- **Trained Response** — 1 — L3 — Once per surge, the frame’s first action costs 0 Momentum.

- **Second Graft** — 1 — L3 — The frame holds two Grown for This benefits at once this scene.

- **Stampede Line** — 1 — L4 — The frame charges through enemies: each in its path takes 2 hits and is pushed aside.

- **Vat-Yard Triage** — 1 — L4 — One-Shot — Fully restore the frame between scenes, or return it to play mid-scene at half HP.

- **The Whistle They Know** — 1 — L5 — Once per session: a second frame answers from somewhere plausible — a loaner, a stray, a friend’s — for the scene.

- **The Yard’s Best Line** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — This scene the frame acts once per surge at 0 Momentum, and something in it is clearly, briefly, choosing to.

- **Grown Together** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — For a scene, you and the frame are one working animal: share HP pools, act as one on your actions, and every drover on the frontier would pay to watch.

## Sawbone

### The Red Thread

- **Suture** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — Heal an adjacent ally 2 HP.

- **Field Surgery** — 1 — L1 — Heal an adjacent ally 3 HP and clear one bleed, poison, or injury.

- **Pressure Here** — 1 — L1 — Reaction — An ally within 3 tiles ignores one bleed or ongoing-damage effect this round.

- **Reading the Body** — 1 — L1 — Diagnose anyone in sight: HP state, afflictions, cause, and one exploitable weakness.

- **Steady the Hands** — 1 — L1 — Stance — Your healing cards heal +1 while you weren’t attacked since your last surge.

- **I Know Where It Hurts** — 1 — L2 — Your Strike targets anatomy: on any hit, the target also loses 1 die from its next action.

- **Clamp and Carry** — 1 — L2 — Stabilize a downed ally and move them your Move in one action.

- **Local Anesthetic** — 1 — L2 — An ally ignores wound penalties and pain effects for a scene; the bill comes due after.

- **Triage** — 1 — L3 — Heal 2 HP split among up to 3 allies within 3 tiles.

- **Case Notes** — 1 — L3 — Against a creature type you’ve treated or dissected: your side’s attacks gain +1 die vs it this scene.

- **Counter-Surgery** — 1 — L4 — Undo flesh-work: end one graft, mutation-effect, or somatic working on a willing or held subject.

- **The Steady Table** — 1 — L4 — Stance — Allies adjacent to you can’t be reduced below 1 HP while you have Momentum unspent on your side.

- **Anatomist’s Strike** — 1 — L5 — Strike or guide an ally’s attack: it ignores Armor; you called the seam.

- **Not On My Table** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — An ally at 0 HP or dead this scene is stabilized at 1 HP; the injury annex gains an entry with your name on it.

- **The Procedure That Gets Named** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — With an hour and a table: repair one thing medicine says is past repair — the ruined hand, the blinded eye, the failing organ. Documented, replicated, entered. Someone paid for this once; today it pays back.

### The Preparatory

- **Tincture Kit** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×2) — Consumable ×3 — Spend to heal 2 HP or clear fatigue.

- **Poultice** — 1 — L1 — Heal an adjacent ally 2 HP; +1 more at the next rest.

- **Wakeleaf** — 1 — L1 — Consumable ×2 — Spend to clear sleep, daze, or stun instantly.

- **Field Brewing** — 1 — L1 — With local plants and an hour: restock one spent Consumable card of yours.

- **Bitter Draught** — 1 — L1 — An ally gains +2 dice on their next save vs poison or disease.

- **Vigor Draught** — 1 — L2 — Consumable ×2 — Spend for +2 dice on one physical roll; the crash is a role-playing opportunity.

- **Numbing Salve** — 1 — L2 — An ally ignores 1 hit from each source for a round.

- **Green Chain Standards** — 1 — L2 — Stance — Your Consumables heal or grant +1 more; you can identify any preparation by smell.

- **Smelling Salts & Worse** — 1 — L3 — Reaction — An ally dropping to 0 HP stays at 1 and acts normally this round.

- **Slow-Release** — 1 — L3 — A dosed ally heals 1 HP at the start of each of your side’s surges this scene.

- **The Traveling Case** — 1 — L4 — Stance — Your hand size counts Consumable cards as half a card each (round up) for reload purposes.

- **Antitoxin, Broad** — 1 — L4 — Consumable ×2 — Spend to end any poison, venom, or spore effect on the bearer or an adjacent ally.

- **Battle Brew** — 1 — L5 — Consumable — Spend: +1 die on all rolls for a scene; afterward, sleep like the honest dead.

- **The Season’s Brew** — 2 — L5 — One-Shot — Before a scene: every ally drinks — +1 die on all saves and +2 max HP for the scene.

- **The Master’s Preparation** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — Produce the legendary batch: one draught that duplicates any single card effect of L5 or below in this book, drinkable by anyone, once.

### The Toxicologist

- **Coated Edge** — 1 — L1 — (Dup ×3) — A weapon’s next 2 hits also cost the target 1 die from its next action.

- **Know the Antidote** — 1 — L1 — Reaction — Cancel one poison, venom, or toxin effect within reach; identify its source.

- **Sleep Dust** — 1 — L1 — Thrown, 3 tiles: one target saves or is drowsy (–1 die, –1 Move) for 2 rounds.

- **Taster’s Immunity** — 1 — L1 — Stance — You are immune to ingested and contact poisons; you can safely taste-test anything.

- **Dose the Drink** — 1 — L1 — Out of combat: lace food or drink undetectably with any preparation you carry.

- **Weakening Agent** — 1 — L2 — Touch or hit: the target’s Strike and Shoot drop 1 for the scene (save resists).

- **Nettle Cloud** — 1 — L2 — 1-tile burst at 3 tiles: enemies there take –1 die on their next roll and cough loudly.

- **Custom Blend** — 1 — L2 — Choose your poison’s rider for the scene: your Coated Edge also slows (–1 Move), sickens (–1 die saves), or marks (glows faintly).

- **The Slow Argument** — 1 — L4 — Touch or hit: the target’s dice pools drop 1 at the start of each of its surges (max –3) until treated.

- **Paralytic** — 1 — L3 — Against a target already poisoned by you: it saves or loses its next action.

- **Mithridatic Regimen** — 1 — L3 — Dose an ally over a rest: +2 dice vs poison and disease for a session.

- **Read the Symptoms** — 1 — L4 — From observation alone: learn exactly what afflicts a creature and precisely what will fix or worsen it.

- **Blight the Blade-Line** — 1 — L5 — Your side’s weapons are dosed this scene: everyone’s first hit each round carries your Coated Edge rider.

- **The Numbers Person** — 2 — L6 — One-Shot — Declare a creature type studied this session: your side’s attacks vs it gain +1 die and ignore 1 Armor for the scene.

- **The Perfect Dose** — 2 — L7 — One-Shot — One creature, one preparation, one outcome, chosen: sleep for hours, truth for minutes, stillness for a scene, or death over days — your call, its save, your conscience.
