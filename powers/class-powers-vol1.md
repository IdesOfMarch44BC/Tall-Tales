# Class Power Lists, Volume 1

**Power format:** **Name** — PL # — Requires Alignment # — tags — effect. Mixed requirements join Alignments with a plus sign. The required dots total the PL; PL 0 powers require none. Using a power does not erase it from the known list.

### The Living Iron

- **Iron Fist** — PL 0 — Requires None — Strike with +1 Attack Die; deal +1 Damage against metal or construct targets.

- **Take the Charge** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Absorb a vim source and bank 3 ebbs (maximum 6).

- **Discharge Through the Hands** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Spend any number of banked ebbs: a touched creature takes Damage N Electricity, where N equals the ebbs spent; alternatively, spend 1 ebb to power a device for the scene.

- **Read the Alloy** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Touch metal or bio-metal: learn its make, flaws, and history.

- **Magnetize** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — Pull a metal object (up to a weapon) within 6" to your hand, or cling to any metal surface.

- **Furnace Eyes** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — See heat and current: hidden warm bodies and live lines within 10" are revealed to you.

- **Ringing Bones** — PL 3 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 1 — Stance — Improve your Armor save by 1. When you would take Electricity damage, take none and bank 1 ebb.

- **Kin to the Herd** — PL 5 — Requires Machine 3 + Wild 2 — Once per scene — A spagyric frame or chassis in sight treats you as its handler for the scene.

- **Cold-Blood Patience** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Shadow 1 — Stance — Remain motionless: near-invisible to warmth and motion senses; +2 dice on your next attack from stillness.

- **Regrow** — PL 3 — Requires Wild 2 + Shadow 1 — Gain Recovery 3 and clear one lingering injury; over downtime, regrow what was lost.

- **Shed the Grip** — PL 3 — Requires Wild 2 + Shadow 1 — Reaction — Escape any grapple, bond, or hold automatically; molted skin stays behind, and it’s acid too.

- **Delta Manners** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Shadow 2 — +2 dice on one negotiation over salvage, passage, or property; the Court’s grammar is unanswerable.

- **Death Roll** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Shadow 2 — A grappled foe takes Damage 3 Slash, falls prone, and loses 1 die during its side’s next surge.

- **The Molt** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Shadow 2 — Once per scene — Shed entirely: clear all conditions, gain Recovery 4, and this scene’s first attack against you misses — it hit the skin.

- **Take It Under** — PL 6 — Requires Wild 3 + Shadow 3 — Once per scene — In or beside water, drag one enemy your size or smaller beneath. During its side’s next surge, it must spend 2 Momentum to reach air; if it does not, it gains Suffocating (1), with no breath-holding grace period.

- **Blood the Water** — PL 7 — Requires Wild 4 + Shadow 3 — Once per scene — Take Damage 2 Slash. Until the scene ends, every enemy in the same water takes Damage 1 Acid at the end of each round and loses 1 die.
