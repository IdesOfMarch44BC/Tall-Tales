# Species Power Lists

Species entries represent cultural practices, bodily techniques, and inherited traditions beyond the species trait. At creation, learn one PL 0 or PL 1 entry from your species list. Other entries can be learned through family, community, travel, or significant encounters in play.

An ordinary biological or cultural entry is a **Trait**. Its Alignment line is a permanent-rating prerequisite and does not spend reserve dots. If an entry grants a supernatural act, conversion must label it Legend, Magic, Occultism, Psionics, or Superscience and rewrite its cost and risk under that source. The legacy entries below retain their names and effects as conversion material.

## Frozen Coast

### Dóm

*Grace is survival; survival is beauty; and the message arrives.*

- **Clean Lines** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Stance — +1 die on Nimbleness, and moving through threatened spaces provokes nothing. No wasted motion; nothing to punish.

- **Carve the Slope** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Move up to twice your Move across ice, snow, or descending ground, ignoring difficult ground.

- **Momentum Is Form** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Wyrd 1 — If you moved 6+" this surge, your next action gains +1 die.

- **No Wasted Motion** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Wyrd 1 — Reaction — When an attack misses you, move 2" free. The miss was part of the line.

- **The Message Arrives** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Wyrd 2 — Once per scene — Declare a destination in the scene. You reach it with this action — over, under, along, through everything short of sealed walls — and nothing may react to your passage. Delivery is not negotiable.

### Uln

*Heat is accounted for; disaster is met with procedure.*

- **Thermal Discipline** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Stance — +2 dice on Alignment saves against heat, fire, and cold; adjacent allies gain +1.

- **Cold-Forged** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Tune an ally’s gear at the molecular grain: their weapon gains +1 Attack Die or their Armor save improves by 1 until the end of the scene (the benefit fails if the item is exposed to intense heat).

- **Measured Twice** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — Before making a Knack or Study roll, learn its required successes and one consequence of failure, then roll with +1 die.

- **Emergency Procedure** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — Reaction — When a structural or environmental hazard endangers your side, negate its effects on allies within 4" this round. Practiced. Coordinated. No panic.

- **Let It Cool** — PL 4 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 2 — Once per scene — Extinguish every fire and heat effect within 6"; constructs and metal-bodied enemies there run cold: –1 die for the scene.

### Wyr

*Precise, recursive, and built for somewhere; memory is what endures.*

- **Recursive Mind** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Reroll all 1s on a Study or Investigate roll.

- **Recorded** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Recall any scene you have witnessed with perfect fidelity; ask the GM one question of detail about it and receive a true answer.

- **Reboot** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wyrd 1 — Clear one mental, fear, or confusion effect from yourself and gain Recovery 1: a controlled restart, mid-fight, eyes open.

- **Cold Clarity** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wyrd 1 — Stance — In cold environments, +1 die on all Knowledge-group rolls. In hot environments, using this power costs 1 additional Momentum, and you resent it.

- **The Mark Wakes** — PL 5 — Requires Machine 3 + Wyrd 2 — Once per scene — Your Maker-mark flares with original intent: negate one supernatural effect targeting you entirely, *or* take 3 automatic successes on one roll. No one knows what the mark was for. Perhaps this was it.

## Salt Reach

### Gorûn

*Every pound is preparation; violence is the last tool in the pack.*

- **Living Reserve** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Draw on what you carry within: you or an adjacent ally clears thirst and fatigue; out of combat, gain Recovery 2 as well.

- **Broad Back** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Stance — Carry an ally or a heavy load at no penalty; +1 die on grapples while you do.

- **The Route Remembered** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — In country you have crossed before: navigation automatically succeeds, and your party treats travel hazards as one step gentler.

- **Last Resort** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Usable only after endurance or negotiation has honestly failed (GM agrees): your first Strike this fight gains +3 dice. The desert taught you exactly when.

- **Written on the Body** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Heavens 2 — Once per scene — Touch the tattoo of an ordeal survived: for the scene, +2 dice on all rolls against a threat of the same kind. Permanence is reserved for truths that will not change.

### Serekh

*Slow to move, impossible to uproot; water is shared or it is nothing.*

- **Stored Against Drought** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Provide water for the whole party for a day, or grant yourself Recovery 2.

- **Rooted** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Stance — You cannot be forcibly moved or made prone, and gain +1 Defend against an attacker that moved at least 3" toward you during the current surge.

- **Living Barricade** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Reaction — An attack on an adjacent smaller ally targets you instead; the attacker takes Damage 1 Pierce.

- **Patient as Stone** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Bank 1 Momentum from this surge; add it after your side rolls Momentum on its next surge. This is an explicit exception to the no-banking rule.

- **Wilt-Sight** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Heavens 2 — Once per scene — Take Damage 2 Toxic through ritual desiccation, then ask the GM three questions about water, weather, or what lies beneath or ahead. Receive true answers.

### Tikka

*Size is a disadvantage only for those who failed to plan.*

- **Always a Bolt-Hole** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Reaction — When targeted while adjacent to cover, a crevice, or diggable ground: +2 Defend against that attack.

- **Scavenged Mechanism** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Place a trap in an adjacent 2-inch space; the first enemy entering takes Damage 2 of an appropriate physical type.

- **False Chamber** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Shadow 1 — Misdirection: one enemy wastes its next action on you-that-isn’t, or moves where you intended (a save resists).

- **Warren Instincts** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Shadow 1 — Stance — +1 die on Perceive against ambushes; underground or indoors, your side cannot be surprised.

- **The Long Preparation** — PL 5 — Requires Machine 3 + Shadow 2 — Once per scene — Reveal that you prepared this ground: place 3 traps (Damage 2 each) anywhere within 10" you plausibly had access to. You did. Obviously you did.

## Verdant Belt

### Brindel

*The thicket is not chaos; it is a network you built.*

- **Thicket-Born** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Stance — Dense vegetation is normal ground for you and grants +1 die to hide; an enemy pursuing you through it takes Damage 1 Slash once per round.

- **Snare-Craft** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Weave a thorn-vine snare in an adjacent 2-inch space: the first enemy entering is held (2 successes to escape).

- **Eye for Small Wonders** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Machine 1 — Once per scene, automatically find one hidden, small, or overlooked thing in your surroundings — the game trail, the loose stone, the beetle worth more than the strongbox.

- **Read the Undergrowth** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Machine 1 — Learn the truth of this land from its small life: what passed, what nests, what’s wrong here. GM answers honestly.

- **Ambush Ecology** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Machine 2 — Once per scene — Make a 6-inch area of vegetation hostile ground for the scene: it is difficult ground for enemies, which take Damage 1 Slash at the end of each round spent inside.

### Lemari

*The canopy is a country; silence means something is wrong.*

- **Canopy Route** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Climb and brachiate at full Move; one ally you guide ignores vertical obstacles on this move too.

- **Many Voices** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Stance — Your layered calls knit the side together: allies within 10" gain +1 die on any roll they make with help, and your side’s coordination cannot be disrupted by noise.

- **Third Hand** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Your tail works while your hands are full: draw, stow, pass, or use an item as a free action; the held item cannot be disarmed.

- **Song Across Branches** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Relay what you perceive: one ally in earshot gains your Perceive result, or +2 dice on their next Perceive.

- **Fruit of the Home Tree** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Heavens 2 — Once per scene, Consumable — Eat the fruit grown from your ancestors: close every minor wound, reduce every moderate wound one step, and clear every condition. It is replaced only at the clan-tree.

### Silvaen

*Grown by imitation; rooted by identity; moral by hard thought.*

- **Mirrored Form** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — +2 dice to pass as your mimicked type; while motionless among plants you are, functionally, one of them.

- **Rooted Recovery** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Feet in soil, unmoving this round: gain Recovery 3.

- **Learn the Shape** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Wyrd 1 — Watch a creature perform a physical feat: perform it yourself this scene at –1 die. (Your Master Mimics trait sharpens this further where skills apply.)

- **Bark and Heartwood** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Wyrd 1 — Stance — Improve your Armor save by 1. Burn attacks against you gain +1 Attack Die.

- **The Forest Learns to Care** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Wyrd 2 — Once per scene — Choose an adjacent ally: improve their Armor save by 2 for the scene or grant them Recovery 3; learn one true thing about what shaped them.

## High Cradle

### Chyrr

*Complacency is a moral failure; the ambush is mercy done efficiently.*

- **Always Ready** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Stance — Once per scene, when your side is surprised, you alone are not: take 1 free action before the enemy surge resolves.

- **Four-Armed Economy** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Draw, reload, stow, and swap as free actions; brace a shield in the lower arms while both uppers work.

- **Strike First** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Shadow 1 — Against an enemy that has not yet acted this scene: +2 dice. Hesitation multiplies risk.

- **Measured Withdrawal** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Shadow 1 — Reaction — When your side’s surge ends, move 2" free, provoking nothing. An enemy routed is no longer relevant; neither is one you’ve stepped away from.

- **The Khet’kha Answer** — PL 5 — Requires Machine 3 + Shadow 2 — Once per scene — Use at the start of an encounter your side begins hidden or prepared: on your first surge, every ally’s first attack gains +1 die and resolves before any enemy Reaction. Sudden. Overwhelming. Brief.

### Korr

*Forty years, spent on purpose.*

- **Sprint the Sky** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Fly up to twice your fly speed this action (it burns against your Athletics flight limit as normal); land at the end, hungry.

- **Forget Nothing** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Recall anything you have personally witnessed, ever; ask the GM one question about a past scene and get the true answer.

- **Burning Hours** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Wyrd 1 — Spend the metabolism: +1 die on any roll — then eat or rest a beat, or take –1 die next round.

- **Watch It Whole** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Wyrd 1 — Stance — From 4+" of height: +2 dice on Perceive, and allies gain +1 die against enemies you can see and call. Height is epistemology.

- **The Time Remaining** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Wyrd 2 — Once per scene — Choose to spend it: take 2 full actions this surge for 1 Momentum total. It costs what it costs; you can feel the sunrises in it; the elders would understand, and would not approve, and would remember.

### Squar

*Anything fully known will eventually be hunted; strength is kept in private.*

- **Leave No Sign** — PL 1 — Requires Shadow 1 — Stance — Your passage erases itself: you cannot be tracked by mundane means, and you leave no trace others can be blamed for.

- **The Hidden Cache** — PL 1 — Requires Shadow 1 — Once per session, in wild country: declare a cache you left here, years back — supplies, a serviceable weapon, or shelter for the party. It was always there.

- **Strength in Private** — PL 2 — Requires Shadow 1 + Wild 1 — While no stranger observes you: +2 dice on any Strike, lift, or feat of raw power. What the myth doesn’t see, the myth can’t hunt.

- **Half Rumor** — PL 2 — Requires Shadow 1 + Wild 1 — Reaction — When enemies first see you this scene: the stories arrive before you do — their side’s next Momentum roll takes –1.

- **Break the Myth** — PL 5 — Requires Shadow 3 + Wild 2 — Once per scene — Be seen fully and deliberately: every enemy who can see you must make a Wild save or cannot act during its side’s next surge. Your Reputation changes permanently.

## Iron Reach

### Barudrim

*A life without deeds worth retelling is a life unfinished; risking others for pride is unforgivable.*

- **Saga-Worthy** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Declare the deed aloud before attempting it: +1 die on the roll. If it fails, refund the Wild dot spent on this power; the story is still told.

- **Shieldbearer** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Reaction — Move one hit from an attack against an adjacent ally onto yourself; resolve it against your defenses instead.

- **Laugh at Fear** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Stance — You and allies within 6" gain +2 dice against fear and despair; also, you are singing, and nobody is sneaking anywhere.

- **Carry Them Out** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Lift an ally — downed included — and move your full Move. The old rest. The young grow. That was always the point of the strength.

- **The Unfinished Life** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Heavens 2 — Once per scene, Reaction — When you become Dying, stabilize immediately, reduce the responsible wound one severity, and stand. If the fight is worthy, you may hear the verse being composed.

### Human

*Rarely the strongest. Reliably the last to give up.*

- **Second Wind** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Gain Recovery 2 and clear fatigue. Once per scene; the well is deep, not bottomless.

- **Fast Learner** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — After failing a roll, gain +2 dice on your next attempt at the same task. The second try is the human art form.

- **Jack of the Frontier** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Treat one skill you have 0 rating in as rating 2 for the scene. You’ve watched this done. How hard can it be. (Hard. Do it anyway.)

- **Persistence Hunter** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Stance — Against anything fleeing you: +1" Move, and your side’s pursuit rolls gain +1 die. You will not tire first. You have never tired first.

- **One More Mile** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Heavens 2 — Once per scene — The oldest human trick, worked on your friends: your whole side clears fatigue, gains Recovery 1, and gains +1" Move for a scene — because you kept going, and made stopping embarrassing.

### Mairin

*Stability is survival; grief ripples; someone must carry the preparations.*

- **The Deeper Pack** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Once per scene, produce the *specific* mundane item this moment needs, worth up to 3 Rings. (Your species trait covers the everyday; this is the once-a-crisis miracle of foresight.)

- **The Careful Larder** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Consumable ×3 — Preserved rations, properly done: spend to gain Recovery 1 and clear hunger or fatigue.

- **Measured Risk** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Heavens 1 — Before an ally rolls: warn them what you see. They may abandon the action (Momentum refunded) or proceed with +1 die.

- **Steady the Ripple** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Heavens 1 — Reaction — When fear, grief, or a morale effect strikes an ally: negate it. You take –1 die on your next roll; you are carrying it, the way your people always have.

- **Everything in Its Place** — PL 5 — Requires Machine 3 + Heavens 2 — Once per scene — The party gains three preparation tokens, each spendable once for Recovery 2, +1 die on a roll, or negating one environmental hazard.

### Nol

*The mask was a prayer before it was a threat; fear, carefully cultivated, is a shield.*

- **The Mask Forward** — PL 1 — Requires Shadow 1 — Stance — +2 dice on Intimidate, and predators of the unseen world — things that hunt by familiarity, name, or memory — take –1 die targeting you. The old purpose still works.

- **Ritual Laughter** — PL 1 — Requires Shadow 1 — You and allies within 6" gain +1 die against fear and despair; enemies hear it and are invited to wonder what, exactly, is funny.

- **Loom Larger** — PL 2 — Requires Shadow 1 + Wyrd 1 — Your silhouette does the arguing: one enemy saves or will not willingly come within 4" of you until you attack it.

- **We Are Not Unreasonable** — PL 2 — Requires Shadow 1 + Wyrd 1 — Deliver terms from behind the mask: enemy morale rolls to keep fighting take –1 die for the scene, and any surrender your side accepts, your side honors. That second clause is the whole reputation.

- **Unmasked** — PL 5 — Requires Shadow 3 + Wyrd 2 — Once per scene — Before one sapient creature, remove the mask and speak honestly. Its hostility ends unless it succeeds on a Shadow save; either way, your Reputation changes among those who hear of it.

### Pelorim

*Identity is the routes you keep; forgetting a path is a moral failure.*

- **The Way Is Known** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Declare you have walked this kind of route: navigation automatically succeeds and the party gains +1 die against its characteristic hazards.

- **Courier’s Gait** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Stance — +1" Move, and a rider you carry may act freely while you move: you are the road and the wagon both.

- **Never Linger** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Reaction — Against anything that would hold, root, or slow you: save with +2 dice, and on success, move 2" free. Stillness is how ways die.

- **The Wider Web** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Once per session: name someone along the routes who owes the ways a favor — a station master, a Gorûn elder, a Warden at a relay. The GM makes them real and reachable.

- **Forward** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Heavens 2 — Once per scene — Invoke the oldest obligation: for one scene, your side ignores difficult ground and gains +1" Move while moving *toward* its goal — and you cannot retreat while it holds. The routes go one direction. That was always the deal.

## Table Notes

**Five powers, one thesis.** Each list states its people’s argument in use: the Dóm list is grace spent on others’ urgencies, the Squar list is power hidden until hiding costs too much, and the human list is five flavors of *not stopping*. When these powers come up at the table, the culture is present in the room—which is why the species lists stay small: seasoning, not the meal.

**The capstones are choices, not upgrades.** Nearly every PL 4–5 signature asks the character to spend something the culture holds dear — the Korr’s hours, the Squar’s myth, the Nol’s mask, the Mairin’s composure, the Serekh’s own body. Players who reach for them should feel the species chapter looking over their shoulder, and GMs should let the aftermath land: *Break the Myth* and *Unmasked* write to the Reputation line in ink.

**Learning beyond creation.** A species list is not automatically known whole. Teachers, kin, rites, and cultural participation can add its powers to a character’s known list when the PL is no higher than half the character’s level, rounded up.
