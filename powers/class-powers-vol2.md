# Class Power Lists, Volume 2

> **Legacy conversion bank:** The entries below preserve names, Alignment footprints, and effect ideas, but they are not playable under the five-source rules as written. Fold Mentalist entries into broad Psionic techniques and result ladders; do not treat the old Mind Blade grouping as a current Mentalist class feature, because Mind Weapon now belongs to the Bounty Hunter. Convert Tinkerer and Sawbone entries into Superscience construction principles or fabricated devices. The universal activation assumption is obsolete.

## Mentalist

For Mentalist power references, **Edge powers** are powers printed under *The Mind Blade*, and **Eye powers** are powers printed under *The Watching Mind*.

### The Mind Blade

- **Drawn Will** — PL 0 — Requires None — Make a mind-blade Strike with +1 Attack Die.

- **First Word** — PL 1 — Requires Wyrd 1 — Stance, Psychic — Your manifested mind blade deals +1 Damage and ignores 1 Defend.

- **Perfect Draw** — PL 1 — Requires Wyrd 1 — Reaction — Manifest and Strike an enemy that just entered your reach.

- **The Form Holds** — PL 1 — Requires Wyrd 1 — Stance — +1 Defend while your blade is manifest.

- **Thrown Intent** — PL 1 — Requires Wyrd 1 — Your blade strikes at 6" and returns to your hand.

- **Cut the Material** — PL 2 — Requires Wyrd 1 + Machine 1 — Your mind-blade Strikes gain AP 2 this round and sever ordinary objects on a hit.

- **Two Truths** — PL 2 — Requires Wyrd 1 + Machine 1 — Split the will: Strike two adjacent enemies at –1 die each.

- **Edge Against the Volley** — PL 2 — Requires Wyrd 1 + Machine 1 — Reaction — Cut one projectile or thrown attack against you out of the air.

- **The Long Cut** — PL 3 — Requires Wyrd 2 + Machine 1 — Strike every enemy in a 4-inch line once.

- **Cut the Working** — PL 4 — Requires Wyrd 2 + Machine 2 — Roll your mind-blade Attack Dice against no Defend to Strike a spell, ward, or supernatural effect within reach; on 2 or more hits, it ends.

- **Cut the Footing** — PL 4 — Requires Wyrd 2 + Machine 2 — Strike the world instead: collapse a surface, drop a beam, open the floor beneath an enemy (save or fall/prone).

- **The Blade Reads Back** — PL 5 — Requires Wyrd 3 + Machine 2 — Cross blades deliberately: learn your opponent’s training, temperament, and next intention.

- **The Opened Way** — PL 6 — Requires Wyrd 3 + Machine 3 — Once per scene — Cut a door in distance: you and adjacent allies step up to 20", through nothing.

- **Cut What Binds** — PL 6 — Requires Wyrd 3 + Machine 3 — Once per scene — Sever one intangible thing within reach: a possession, a compulsion, a curse, a leash. Not a debt. Not yet.

- **The Unnameable Edge** — PL 8 — Requires Wyrd 4 + Machine 4 — Once per scene — Cut something ordinary language does not hold — an inevitability, a connection, a certainty — GM adjudicates; the scriptoria will want your account.

### The Watching Mind

- **Centered Breath** — PL 0 — Requires None — +2 dice on your next Alignment save, or clear a –1 die penalty on yourself.

- **Still Water** — PL 1 — Requires Wyrd 1 — Stance — +2 dice on Alignment saves against fear, deception, and mind-effects; you cannot be surprised.

- **Read the Stance** — PL 1 — Requires Wyrd 1 — One observed creature: learn its intent, its fear, or its next action.

- **Open Hand** — PL 1 — Requires Wyrd 1 — Read a room: every present creature’s mood and honesty, at a glance.

- **Offered Calm** — PL 1 — Requires Wyrd 1 — An adjacent ally gains Still Water’s benefits until your next surge.

- **Speech Without Voice** — PL 2 — Requires Wyrd 1 + Heavens 1 — Until end of scene, your side converses silently at any distance in sight.

- **Unmoved** — PL 2 — Requires Wyrd 1 + Heavens 1 — Reaction — Automatically succeed one save against fear or mental influence.

- **The Noticing** — PL 2 — Requires Wyrd 1 + Heavens 1 — One deception, disguise, or illusion within 10" fails for you specifically.

- **Weight of Attention** — PL 3 — Requires Wyrd 2 + Heavens 1 — Fix one creature in your regard: it takes –1 die on all actions not directed at you.

- **Memory Palace** — PL 3 — Requires Wyrd 2 + Heavens 1 — Store or retrieve anything you’ve perceived with perfect fidelity; share one stored scene with a willing touch.

- **Quiet the Storm** — PL 4 — Requires Wyrd 2 + Heavens 2 — End one rage, panic, or frenzy effect in a creature within 6" (unwilling: it saves).

- **The Shared Stillness** — PL 4 — Requires Wyrd 2 + Heavens 2 — Stance — Allies within 6" gain +1 die on all Alignment saves and cannot be surprised.

- **Going Out** — PL 5 — Requires Wyrd 3 + Heavens 2 — Once per scene — Leave the body for one round: perceive anywhere within the site, unseen, then return. A rehearsal, plainly labeled.

- **The Closed Door, Held Open** — PL 7 — Requires Wyrd 4 + Heavens 3 — Once per scene — With consent, enter a willing mind: clear one mental affliction, share one memory truly, or stand with them against an intruding effect.

- **The Finished Self** — PL 8 — Requires Wyrd 4 + Heavens 4 — Once per scene — For one scene, nothing in you is unexamined: immune to all mental influence, all your Alignment saves succeed on 3+, and you may lend one save automatically to an ally who can see your face.

### The Composed Self

- **Wayfarer’s Step** — PL 0 — Requires None — Move full Move ignoring difficult ground.

- **Traveling Form** — PL 1 — Requires Heavens 1 — Stance — +1" Move; opportunity Strikes against you lose 1 die; your blade manifests and dismisses freely.

- **Beggar’s Bowl** — PL 1 — Requires Heavens 1 — The gate custom in miniature: secure food, shelter, and a hearing anywhere the tradition is known.

- **Correcting Touch** — PL 1 — Requires Heavens 1 — Adjust an ally’s grip mid-action: +1 die on their current roll.

- **Walk On** — PL 1 — Requires Heavens 1 — Reaction — When missed by an attack, move 2"; you were already leaving.

- **A Correction, Offered** — PL 2 — Requires Heavens 1 + Wild 1 — An ally within 6" rerolls up to 2 dice of a roll you watched.

- **Teaching Bout** — PL 2 — Requires Heavens 1 + Wild 1 — Spar an ally a quiet hour: they gain +1 die on one skill for the session.

- **The Road’s Judgment** — PL 2 — Requires Heavens 1 + Wild 1 — Assess a stranger as walkers do: learn their training, their trouble, and whether they’ve sat a gate.

- **The Gate Custom** — PL 3 — Requires Heavens 2 + Wild 1 — Once per scene per creature: an enemy accepting your offered pause stops fighting you until harmed.

- **Carry Word Between Houses** — PL 3 — Requires Heavens 2 + Wild 1 — Once per session: know where the nearest monastery, gate-friend, or fellow walker is, and be expected there.

- **Both Limbs** — PL 4 — Requires Heavens 2 + Wild 2 — Also use one PL 2 or lower Edge power and one PL 2 or lower Eye power you know. They require no additional dots or Momentum; resolve all three effects now.

- **Uninvited Nowhere** — PL 4 — Requires Heavens 2 + Wild 2 — Stance — You can’t be barred by social station: guards hesitate, doors open, courts hear you; the robe precedes you.

- **Learn What We Don’t Know** — PL 5 — Requires Heavens 3 + Wild 2 — Once per scene — Observe any technique used this scene and perform it once now. If it is not already a power, the GM translates it into an immediate effect no stronger than another PL 5 power.

- **The Walker’s Report** — PL 6 — Requires Heavens 3 + Wild 3 — Once per session — The tradition acts on your word: a house alerted, a Sundered watch begun, or a Reading convened where you say.

- **Everywhere the Road Goes** — PL 7 — Requires Heavens 4 + Wild 3 — Once per scene — For one journey, the party moves at walker’s pace: half time, no hazards you don’t choose, and one useful encounter the road owed you.

## Tinkerer

### The Coilwright

- **Jolt** — PL 0 — Requires None — One target within 6" takes Damage 2 Electricity. A Living Iron may ignore it and bank 1 ebb instead; another construct may ignore it and restore 1 Durability instead.

- **Storage Cell** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Consumable ×2 — Spend for +1 Momentum (bearer’s surge) or to power a device for a scene.

- **Patch the Line** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Restore 2 Durability to a device, Heart, or construct; restore basic function to one disabled machine.

- **Static Field** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Stance — An enemy entering within 2" of you takes Damage 1 Electricity, once per round.

- **Kill the Lights** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Cut power in a 10-inch radius: lamps, lines, and one powered device of the GM’s choice go dark.

- **Charged Weapon** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — An ally’s weapon deals +1 Electricity Damage on its next two hits.

- **Capacitor Trap** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — Rig a 2-inch space; the first enemy entering takes Damage 3 Electricity.

- **Ground the Line** — PL 3 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 1 — Reaction — Redirect one vim, lightning, or Heart-driven effect within 6" into the earth; your next power this scene that deals Electricity damage deals +1 Damage.

- **Overvolt** — PL 3 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 1 — Choose a powered device or construct within 6": it takes Damage 3 Electricity, or one of its printed Damage, Move, or restoration values doubles until your side’s next surge; it then becomes disabled until repaired.

- **Jolt-Line Web** — PL 4 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 2 — String live wire across a 6-inch line; enemies crossing take Damage 2 Electricity and end their movement.

- **The Quickened Heart** — PL 4 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 2 — A vehicle, frame, or Heart you tend gains +1" Move and +1 die for the scene.

- **Read the Current** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — Trace any powered system: layout, loads, and where it fails.

- **Rolling Blackout** — PL 5 — Requires Machine 3 + Wild 2 — Enemy devices and constructs within 10" become disabled until your side’s next surge; powered gear there grants no benefit for the same duration.

- **The Dawn Clause** — PL 6 — Requires Machine 3 + Wild 3 — Once per scene — At the procedurally correct moment (the GM agrees the sky is right), triple one printed Damage, Durability restored, distance, or duration number on your next Coilwright power this surge. Do not triple PL, Alignment requirements, targets, powers, or actions.

- **Heart Transplant** — PL 7 — Requires Machine 4 + Wild 3 — Once per scene — Field-swap a Constructive Heart: restore a destroyed great machine to full Durability, or give a functioning one +2 Attack Dice, +2 Damage, and +2" Move for the scene. Afterward it is disabled for a week.

### The Crucible-Trained

- **Flask of Opinion** — PL 0 — Requires None — Thrown 10": creatures in a 2-inch burst take Damage 2; choose Burn, Acid, or Toxic when thrown. The area reflects the choice for the round.

- **Solvent** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Destroy a door’s worth of material, or worsen a target’s Armor save by 2 until repaired.

- **Smoke Pot** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — 4" of dense smoke: Shoot attacks through it lose 2 dice.

- **Litmus Sense** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Identify any substance, poison, or residue by field test.

- **Quickmatch** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Reaction — Ignite, extinguish, or neutralize one small chemical event within 6".

- **Alloy on Demand** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — An ally’s weapon gains +1 Attack Die or their Armor save improves by 1 until the end of the scene.

- **Caustic Coat** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — The next three hits from a weapon each worsen the target’s Armor save by 1 for the scene, after resolving that hit.

- **Flash Powder** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Wild 1 — 2-inch burst: enemies there save or are blinded a round (–2 dice).

- **Transmute the Ground** — PL 3 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 1 — 4" become (choose): mire (difficult), glass (loud, slick), or brittle (collapses under weight).

- **The Stable Batch** — PL 3 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 1 — Consumable ×2 — As Flask of Opinion, but Damage 3; an ally may carry and throw it.

- **Neutralize** — PL 4 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 2 — Reaction — Cancel one acid, fire, poison, or alchemical effect as it lands, anywhere within 6".

- **Refiner’s Eye** — PL 4 — Requires Machine 2 + Wild 2 — Assess any material’s worth, purity, and provenance; spot orichalcum through a crate.

- **The Big Batch** — PL 5 — Requires Machine 3 + Wild 2 — Every enemy in a 4-inch burst within 10" takes Damage 3 Blast.

- **Orichalcum Sliver** — PL 6 — Requires Machine 3 + Wild 3 — Once per scene — Overcharge a weapon: its next attack adds Damage equal to your Study.

- **Philosopher’s Hour** — PL 7 — Requires Machine 4 + Wild 3 — Once per scene — With a bench and an hour: transmute one material into another of like mass (stone to iron, iron to glass, lead to — no. The Crucible has rules about that one).

### The Frame-Wright

- **The Frame** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Troop — Deploy your chassis: Durability 5, Attack 3, Defend 1, Move 4", carries loads; re-musters between encounters.

- **Patch and Graft** — PL 0 — Requires None — Restore 3 Durability to a frame or chassis.

- **Fetch** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — The frame retrieves an object or drags an ally up to its Move.

- **Gentle the Stranger** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Calm any frame, chassis, or beast of burden; +2 dice handling it thereafter.

- **Mounted Work** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Stance — Ride your frame: your Move becomes its Move; you gain +1 Defend from height.

- **Grown for This** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Machine 1 — Stance — Choose the frame’s graft: +1 Attack Die (horn), +1 Defend (plate), or +2" Move (limbs).

- **Good Beast** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Machine 1 — Reaction — The frame interposes: an attack on you or an adjacent ally hits it instead.

- **Haul Away** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Machine 1 — The frame moves double its Move carrying up to two people or a wagon’s load.

- **Trained Response** — PL 3 — Requires Wild 2 + Machine 1 — Once per surge, the frame’s first action costs 0 Momentum.

- **Second Graft** — PL 3 — Requires Wild 2 + Machine 1 — The frame holds two Grown for This benefits at once this scene.

- **Stampede Line** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Machine 2 — The frame Moves in a line; each enemy in its path takes Damage 2 Blunt and is pushed to the nearest open side.

- **Vat-Yard Triage** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Machine 2 — Once per scene — Fully restore the frame between scenes, or return it to use mid-scene at half its maximum Durability, rounded up.

- **The Whistle They Know** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Machine 2 — Once per session: a second frame answers from somewhere plausible — a loaner, a stray, a friend’s — for the scene.

- **The Yard’s Best Line** — PL 6 — Requires Wild 3 + Machine 3 — Once per scene — This scene the frame acts once per surge at 0 Momentum, and something in it is clearly, briefly, choosing to.

- **Grown Together** — PL 7 — Requires Wild 4 + Machine 3 — Once per scene — For the scene, occupy the frame’s space and move together. Before resolving a hit against you, you may remove 1 frame Durability to cancel that hit. Your actions may originate from your position or the frame’s.

## Sawbone

### The Red Thread

- **Suture** — PL 0 — Requires None — Grant an adjacent ally Recovery 2.

- **Field Surgery** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Grant an adjacent ally Recovery 3 and clear one bleed, poison, or injury.

- **Pressure Here** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Reaction — An ally within 6" ignores one bleed or ongoing-damage effect this round.

- **Reading the Body** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Diagnose anyone in sight: wound state, afflictions, cause, and one exploitable weakness.

- **Steady the Hands** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Stance — Powers you use grant +1 Recovery while you have not been attacked since your last surge.

- **I Know Where It Hurts** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Machine 1 — Your Strike targets anatomy: on any hit, the target also loses 1 die from its next action.

- **Clamp and Carry** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Machine 1 — Stabilize a downed ally and move them your Move in one action.

- **Local Anesthetic** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Machine 1 — An ally ignores wound penalties and pain effects for a scene; the bill comes due after.

- **Triage** — PL 3 — Requires Wild 2 + Machine 1 — Distribute Recovery 3 among up to three allies within 6".

- **Case Notes** — PL 3 — Requires Wild 2 + Machine 1 — Against a creature type you’ve treated or dissected: your side’s attacks gain +1 die vs it this scene.

- **Counter-Surgery** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Machine 2 — Undo flesh-work: end one graft, mutation-effect, or somatic working on a willing or held subject.

- **The Steady Table** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Machine 2 — Stance — While your side has unspent Momentum, the first wound each round that would make an adjacent ally Dying is reduced one severity.

- **Anatomist’s Strike** — PL 5 — Requires Wild 3 + Machine 2 — Strike or guide an ally’s attack: it ignores Armor; you called the seam.

- **Not On My Table** — PL 6 — Requires Wild 3 + Machine 3 — Once per scene — A Dying ally within reach is stabilized and the responsible wound is reduced one severity. If they suffered Death since the end of your side’s last surge, change Death to Dying first.

- **The Procedure That Gets Named** — PL 7 — Requires Wild 4 + Machine 3 — Once per scene — With an hour and a table: repair one thing medicine says is past repair — the ruined hand, the blinded eye, the failing organ. Documented, replicated, entered. Someone paid for this once; today it pays back.

- **Poultice** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Grant an adjacent ally Recovery 2; +1 more at the next rest.

- **Wakeleaf** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — Consumable ×2 — Spend to clear sleep, Dazed, or one Disabled effect not caused by Dying.

- **Field Brewing** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — With local plants and an hour, restore one marked use to one Consumable power of yours.

- **Bitter Draught** — PL 1 — Requires Wild 1 — An ally gains +2 dice on their next Alignment save vs poison or disease.

- **Vigor Draught** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Consumable ×2 — Spend for +2 dice on one physical roll; the crash is a role-using opportunity.

- **Numbing Salve** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Until the end of the round, reduce the first attack against the ally by 1 hit, after rolling.

- **Green Chain Standards** — PL 2 — Requires Wild 1 + Heavens 1 — Stance — Your Consumables grant +1 Recovery or +1 bonus die, as appropriate; identify any preparation by smell.

- **Slow-Release** — PL 3 — Requires Wild 2 + Heavens 1 — A dosed ally gains Recovery 1 at the start of each of your side’s surges this scene.

- **The Traveling Case** — PL 4 — Requires Wild 2 + Heavens 2 — Stance — The first time you use a Consumable power each scene, do not mark off one of its uses; drawing, passing, and stowing your preparations are free.

- **The Master’s Preparation** — PL 7 — Requires Wild 4 + Heavens 3 — Once per scene — Produce the legendary batch: one draught that duplicates any single PL 5 or lower power effect in this book, drinkable by anyone, once.

### The Toxicologist

- **Coated Edge** — PL 0 — Requires None — A weapon’s next 2 hits also cost the target 1 die from its next action.

- **Know the Antidote** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Reaction — Cancel one poison, venom, or toxin effect within reach; identify its source.

- **Sleep Dust** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Thrown, 6": one target saves or is drowsy (–1 die, –1" Move) for 2 rounds.

- **Taster’s Immunity** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Stance — You are immune to ingested and contact poisons; you can safely taste-test anything.

- **Dose the Drink** — PL 1 — Requires Machine 1 — Out of combat: lace food or drink undetectably with any preparation you carry.

- **Weakening Agent** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Shadow 1 — On touch or hit, the target makes a Wild save; on failure, worsen its Strike and Shoot target numbers by 1 for the scene.

- **Nettle Cloud** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Shadow 1 — 2-inch burst at 6": enemies there take –1 die on their next roll and cough loudly.

- **Custom Blend** — PL 2 — Requires Machine 1 + Shadow 1 — Choose your poison’s rider for the scene: your Coated Edge also slows (–1" Move), sickens (–1 die saves), or marks (glows faintly).

- **Paralytic** — PL 3 — Requires Machine 2 + Shadow 1 — Against a target already poisoned by you: it saves or loses its next action.

- **Mithridatic Regimen** — PL 3 — Requires Machine 2 + Shadow 1 — Dose an ally over a rest: +2 dice vs poison and disease for a session.

- **The Slow Argument** — PL 4 — Requires Machine 2 + Shadow 2 — Touch or hit: the target’s dice pools drop 1 at the start of each of its surges (max –3) until treated.

- **Read the Symptoms** — PL 4 — Requires Machine 2 + Shadow 2 — From observation alone: learn exactly what afflicts a creature and precisely what will fix or worsen it.

- **Blight the Blade-Line** — PL 5 — Requires Machine 3 + Shadow 2 — Your side’s weapons are dosed this scene: everyone’s first hit each round carries your Coated Edge rider.

- **The Numbers Person** — PL 6 — Requires Machine 3 + Shadow 3 — Once per scene — Declare a creature type studied this session: your side’s attacks against it gain +1 Attack Die and AP 1 for the scene.

- **The Perfect Dose** — PL 7 — Requires Machine 4 + Shadow 3 — Once per scene — One creature, one preparation, one outcome, chosen: sleep for hours, truth for minutes, stillness for a scene, or death over days — your call, its save, your conscience.
